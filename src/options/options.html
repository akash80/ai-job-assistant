Resume

Paste your resume and let AI intelligently extract your profile.

How it works

  1. Copy your entire resume — open your resume (PDF, Word, or any format), select all text (Ctrl+A), and copy it (Ctrl+C).
  2. Paste it below — click the text area and paste (Ctrl+V). Don't worry about formatting — AI understands any layout.
  3. Click "Save & Parse with AI" — the AI will read your resume, extract all data, and auto-fill your entire profile.
  4. Review the preview — you'll see exactly what AI extracted as a structured JSON. Then check the Profile tab to review and edit.

Works with any format: The AI handles hundreds of resume formats — standard headings, creative layouts, different section names, any language.

Nothing is missed: Skills, experience, education, projects, certifications, languages, open source, awards, publications — if it's in your resume, it's extracted.
