# Privacy Policy for AI Job Assistant

Effective date: March 30, 2026

AI Job Assistant ("the extension") is a Chrome extension that helps users analyze job postings and assist with job application form filling.

This policy explains what data is processed, how it is used, and your choices.

## 1) Data We Process

Depending on how you use the extension, the extension may process:

- Website content from pages where you activate the extension (for example, job descriptions and form fields).
- User-provided profile data you enter in the extension (for example, name, email, phone, skills, resume details).
- Your OpenAI API key, if you provide one.
- Basic extension settings and usage state (for example, preferences, cached outputs, counters).

## 2) How Data Is Used

Data is used only to provide the extension's core purpose:

- Analyze job postings.
- Match job requirements with your profile information.
- Generate AI-assisted suggestions.
- Help autofill application forms.
- Save your settings so the extension works across sessions.

## 3) Where Data Is Stored

- Data is stored locally in your browser using Chrome extension storage (`chrome.storage.local`).
- We do not operate our own backend server for storing your extension data.

## 4) Third-Party Services

When you trigger AI features, relevant content may be sent directly from your browser to:

- OpenAI API (`https://api.openai.com/*`) for AI analysis/suggestions.
- Frankfurter API (`https://api.frankfurter.app/*`) for currency conversion features, when used.

Your use of those services is also subject to their own terms and privacy policies.

## 5) Data Sharing and Sale

- We do not sell personal data.
- We do not transfer user data to third parties except as needed to provide the extension's requested functionality (for example, API calls initiated by user actions).
- We do not use user data for advertising, creditworthiness, or lending decisions.

## 6) Tracking and Analytics

- No analytics or telemetry are used for cross-site tracking.
- The extension does not include third-party ad trackers.

## 7) Remote Code

- The extension does not load or execute remotely hosted code.
- Executable code is packaged with the extension.

## 8) Data Retention and Deletion

- Locally stored data remains until you remove it via extension settings, clear browser extension data, or uninstall the extension.
- You can stop data processing by disabling or uninstalling the extension.

## 9) Security

We take reasonable steps to reduce risk, including limiting permissions and keeping processing scoped to extension features. No method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure.

## 10) Children's Privacy

The extension is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from children.

## 11) Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. Material updates will be reflected by changing the effective date above.
